About Arbour Lake

In 1991, the very first Arbour Lake show-home opened it's doors, wowing homebuyers right from the very start. By 1993, the lake was ready for residents to enjoy water activities for the first time ever in the Northwest of Calgary!

Arbour Lake has some of the most amazing residential properties in Northwest Calgary.

This residential neighbourhood is comprised of about 70% single-family dwellings, of which about 87% are owner-occupied. Apartments and townhomes represent about 25% of Arbour Lake homes for sale, giving homebuyers plenty of options to choose from.

Homebuyers will find an abundance of 2+ car garages, big picture windows, and well-maintained yards. Interiors range in styles, where most capitalize on open, airy spaces with giving that play well to today's open-modern styles.

Community Vibe in Arbour Lake

The priveleges of Arbour Lake living include fishing, paddleboarding, and other water sports just minutes from your door.

Located in the northwest quadrant of Calgary, Alberta, this residential neighbourhood is proudly Northwest Calgary's only lake community.

With remarkable mountain views, world-class amenities, and an incredible, picture-perfect lake, there is no shortage of beautiful property in the community of Arbour Lake.

In addition the single-family homes, Arbour Lake also offers townhomes, condos, and beautiful estates, making finding your dream home easy.

Homestyles in Arbour Lake wonderfully reflect the innovative era they were built in. Clean lines with ample space, homebuyers will have a selection of move-in ready homes as well as homes that are ready for your creative touch. Finishes vary from stucco, stone, or brick, or a mix of complementary mediums. Many of the homes are 2-story, offering plenty of room for growing families.
