Its best to call an agent to sell your home within three to six months prior to your list date. When you start talking to an agent several months before wanting to sell, it leaves enough time to find the best agent, be advised of key preparations, and market your home for all its worth, all of which are essential elements in selling for top dollar. Seasonal and cyclical trends in the market can also effect when you want to list, and the typical “list in the spring market” mentality is not exactly correct. The Group recommends that you talk to one of our REALTORS® as soon as you're thinking of listing your home for sale. Generally speaking, that's anywhere from 3 to 6 months prior to your desired move date.

While individual real estate agent commissions do vary, most real estate agents in Calgary work for 7% on the first $100,000 and 3% on the balance of the home sale price. This will typically be split down the middle to be shared between the buyers agent who brings in their buyer clients, and the listing or marketing agent who markets and represents the seller and the listed property. Home sellers in Calgary are fortunate as the real estate fees for selling here are among the lowest in North America, with the typical being a flat 5% and many areas having land transfer taxes on top of that.
