Why do people choose to live in NW Calgary communities?

Simply, Northwest Calgary allows for the best of both worlds:
• The convenience of being able to jump onto the highway and be out of the city, or downtown within minutes.
• The spectacular views of the mountains and the breathtaking Bow River Valley make this area of Calgary a place you will want to call home.

Communities here are a dream for outdoor and sports enthusiasts. Shouldice Athletic Park, McMahon Stadium, and the Olympic Oval are some of the many sports facilities and venues that you will definitely enjoy.

If you are a retiree or want to unwind and reconnect with nature, views of the awe-inspiring mountains are one of the hallmarks of this area, echoed in its scenic elevations and views of the city. Enjoy a short walk at Nose Hill Park - one of the largest city parks in North America.

NorthWest Calgary has so many amenities and hosts many excellent communities. You can start exploring some of the stand-outs like Hill Hurst, Sage Hill, Evanston, Citadel, Arbour Lake, and Tuscany.
